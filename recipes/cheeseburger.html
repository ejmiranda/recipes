Cheeseburger

Description

A piece of heaven made from sun and happiness.

Ingredients

  • Buns
  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Pickles
  • Onion
  • Sauces
  • Optional: Tomato and Lettuce

Steps

  1. Grill meat.
  2. Open buns and spread sauce.
  3. Add meat.
  4. Add chesse.
  5. Add chopped onions and pickles.
  6. Add more sauce.
  7. Close bun and heat on pan.
  8. Eat with your prefered side (Chef's recommendation: French fries).
