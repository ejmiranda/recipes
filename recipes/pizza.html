Pizza

Description

An amazing disc of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Stretch dough into a circular shape.
  3. Spread tomato sauce.
  4. Add cheese (preferably in chunks).
  5. Add pepperoni.
  6. Put in oven until cheese is melted and dough is golden brown.
  7. Slice pizza.
  8. Optionally, dip slice in garlic sauce.
  9. Eat and enjoy!
